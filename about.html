Our Mission: Getting You Paid

We are a dedicated support service for foreign workers in Ireland who have been left with unpaid wages.

Your Advocate for Fair Pay in Ireland

PayClaim Ireland was founded to bridge the gap for foreign workers who face language and administrative barriers when trying to claim money they are rightfully owed. We understand how stressful and confusing it can be. Our team provides clear, multilingual support to prepare your documents, file complaints, and, if needed, connect you with trusted solicitors. We are not a law firm, but we are your first and best step towards justice.

We saw too many hardworking individuals, especially in construction, being taken advantage of simply because they were unfamiliar with the system or didn't speak the language. We created a service to provide the administrative and translation support they need to fight back.

No, PayClaim Ireland is not a law firm and we do not provide legal advice. We offer administrative support, translation, and case preparation. When legal action is necessary, we refer you to a fully licensed and trusted solicitor who specialises in employment law.

Easy 3-Step Process to Claim Your Money

1.

Tell Us Your Story

Contact us via WhatsApp or our form and explain your situation in your own language.

2.
